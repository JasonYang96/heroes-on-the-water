Thank you for your interest in Heroes on the Water. We are dedicated to the physical and emotional rehabilitation of disabled active military service personnel and veterans through the sport of kayak fishing. Heroes on the Water is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit and all donations are tax deductible.

Please consider a recurring donation to automatically give on a monthly basis by clicking on the SUBSCRIBE button to the right. One time gifts can be made by clicking the DONATE NOW button below.

Our nation’s warriors face a new battle as they return home. You can give them and their families peace and hope for a brighter future by making a donation today. Your gift provides the HOW kayak fishing program to warriors at no charge to them or their families.

Contributions can be directed to a HOW Chapter by entering the chapter name in the PayPal message field in the secure checkout or in the check’s memo field.

Thank you for choosing to support those we serve.




Corporate Matching Gifts

Corporate matching gifts are a great way to maximize a personal contribution to increase the impact of your gift. By tapping into an employer’s matching gift benefit, donation amounts may double or even triple!

How do I get my gift matched?

First, find out if your company has a matching gift program.(Corporate Match List) Each company has its own guidelines for employees, spouses, retirees, and widows/widowers. Most corporate procedures are simple:

  • Request a Matching Form from Human Resources or your company website.
  • Complete and submit the form to your employer or HOW, according to the directions on the form.
  • HOW will verify the form with the company.
  • Company issues a matching gift contribution to HOW.
